CREDO
Dallas' Community Choir

  • Fully Responsive Design

  • Google Maps Integration

  • Redesigned Home Page

I helped CREDO change and update the design of their old website at no charge. They are a non-profit ecumenical choir in Dallas, TX. Jonathan Palant (CREDO's director/conductor) was interested in keeping the same basic content on the site while making it easier to use. Since CREDO already had Wordpress installed, we worked together to select a new theme, coloring, and overall style.
